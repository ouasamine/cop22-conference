COP22 Marrakech
Global Climate Conference 2016

The main objective of the annual Conference of Parties (COP) is to review the ‘Rio Convention’ that included the adoption of the UN Framework on Climate Change (UNFCCC) entering into force in 1994 and now has a near-universal membership of 196 countries.
This convention set out a framework for action aimed avoiding “dangerous anthropogenic interference with the climate system.”
